About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Insurance

At Tricity Insurance, we’ve been safeguarding families and businesses for over 25 years with tailored insurance solutions. Our commitment to transparency, reliability, and personalized service ensures you have the coverage you need to thrive.

Founded in 1998, Tricity Insurance began with a simple goal: to provide dependable insurance solutions that give peace of mind.

Our journey is built on trust, innovation, and a deep understanding of our clients’ needs, making us a leader in the insurance industry.

Our mission is to protect what matters most to you with customized insurance plans backed by exceptional service and unwavering integrity.

We strive to make insurance simple, transparent, and accessible, ensuring every client feels confident in their coverage.

We envision a world where everyone has access to reliable insurance that empowers them to live and work without fear of the unexpected.

By leveraging technology and expertise, we aim to redefine the insurance experience for generations to come.
